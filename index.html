Your ESE Becomes a TV Show

Every week your team creates short-form content — TikTok-length clips, behind-the-scenes footage, event previews. At the end of the programme, your ESE submits a 10-minute episode to Blastbeat TV.

Weekly Episodes

Competition Pathway

The best episodes compete at National Finals. Your content is your pitch. Your story is your brand.
