The 4 Phases

A structured journey from idea to impact — in four powerful phases.

01
Weeks 1–4

Foundation & Identity

Form your ESE. Elect your leadership. Assign all 14 roles. Build your brand identity and social media presence. Set goals and define your event concept.

02
Weeks 5–8

Build & Sell

Launch ticket sales. Book talent. Secure venue. Build your content engine. Begin weekly Blastbeat TV episodes and grow your audience before the event.

03
Weeks 9–12

Event & Impact

Run your real event. Collect revenue, manage the floor, debrief your team. Film the experience for your TV episode submission and impact report.

04
Weeks 13–16

Finance, CAP & Finals

Distribute 75% profit to your team. Allocate 25% to your Climate Action Project. Submit your TV episode. Compete at Regional and National Finals.
