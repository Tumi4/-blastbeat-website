our vision

Where Music Meets Movement

At Blastbeat, we envision a world where music serves as a catalyst for change, empowering youth to become innovative leaders.

🎵

Music & Arts

Concerts, talent shows, and creative events that give young people a platform to express themselves and build confidence through performance.

⚽

Sports Enterprise

Tournaments, sports days, and athletic events that combine teamwork on the field with business skills off it.

🌱

Climate Action

25% of all ESE profits fund student-designed climate projects. Real environmental impact, driven by the next generation.

📈

Real Business Skills

Finance, marketing, HR, legal, sales — students fill 14 real business roles and manage real money. No simulations.

📅 our journey

23 Years of Youth-Led Change

2003 THE BEGINNING

Founded in Ireland

Robert Stephenson launches Blastbeat in Dublin, pioneering the idea that music events can teach young people real business skills and social responsibility.

2008 GOING GLOBAL

Japan Operations Begin

Blastbeat expands to Japan, proving the model works across cultures. Young people in Tokyo and beyond start running their own Event Social Enterprises.

2020 AFRICA

South Africa Pilot

Blastbeat launches its first pilot programme in South Africa, adapting the model for the African context and laying the groundwork for continental expansion.

2025 BREAKTHROUGH YEAR

Web Summit Selected & Namibia NIPDB Partnership

Blastbeat is selected for Web Summit 2025, gaining international recognition. A landmark partnership with the Namibia Investment Promotion and Development Board (NIPDB) opens new doors for African expansion.

2026 NOW

App Launch & National Finals Cape Town

The Blastbeat platform goes live, enabling schools across South Africa to run the full programme digitally. The inaugural National Finals will take place in Cape Town, showcasing the best ESE teams in the country.
