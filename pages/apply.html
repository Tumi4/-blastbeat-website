What's Included

Everything Your School Needs to Launch

No licensing cost for founding schools. No hidden extras. Just the tools, training, and support to deliver world-class enterprise skills education.

Full Programme Licence

Access to the complete MACC and/or FootBeat curriculum — digital lesson plans, assessment tools, and student workbooks.

Teacher Training & CPD

In-school onboarding + live virtual workshops. Your educators become certified ESE facilitators with ongoing professional development hours.

National Competition Entry

Students compete at regional and national Blastbeat showcases — pitching business ideas and performing for industry panels, with prizes and media coverage.

CAPS-Aligned Curriculum

The programme maps to South African CAPS requirements for Business Studies, EMS, Life Orientation, and Arts — no extra admin for your HODs.

Dedicated School Dashboard

Track student progress, log completion, and generate reports for SGB or donor reporting — all from your school's Blastbeat admin portal.
