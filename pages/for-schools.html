FootBeat · for sports clubs

For School And Community Sports Clubs.

Blastbeat also powers FootBeat, our format for football and other sports. Designed for young people aged 14–34 in school and community sports clubs.

Players form a real Event Social Enterprise around a tournament, showcase match, skills day or local festival. They take on roles like captain-CEO, finance, marketing, media and climate lead, plan the event, sell tickets and secure local sponsors. Teams keep 75% of profits to reinvest in kit, travel or development, and direct 25% into a climate project — greener pitches, boot recycling, tree planting, water-saving initiatives.

Young athletes build leadership, financial literacy, communication and media skills while strengthening club culture.
