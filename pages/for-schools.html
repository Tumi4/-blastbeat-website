Three Steps to Launch

From initial conversation to students running real events — we handle the complexity.

1
2–3 weeks before term

Initial Consultation

Schedule a no-obligation call. We’ll assess your school’s needs and plan the programme rollout together. No pressure, no jargon.

2
1–2 weeks before launch

Teacher Training

Two dedicated training sessions (online or in-person) covering the platform, curriculum, and facilitation techniques. Full certification provided.

3
Beginning of term

Programme Launch

Students form ESEs and begin. The platform handles team management, task delivery, progress tracking, and competition entry automatically.

🚀 the platform

Technology Built for Schools

Every Blastbeat school gets access to a purpose-built digital platform that runs the whole programme — from student onboarding to impact reporting.

👥

Teacher Dashboard

Live student registration list, role assignments, and team overview. The platform auto-calculates the current programme week and phase — no manual tracking needed.

Live progress Role assignment Phase control
🤖

Built-In AI Mentor

Every student has a personal AI mentor embedded in the platform. They can ask it anything — what their role involves, how to complete a task, ideas for their event. No external tools needed.

Per-student AI chat Role guidance Team chat
📱

Mobile-First

Designed for students who live on their phones. The full platform — student dashboard, tasks, team chat, and teacher controls — works flawlessly on mobile and tablet.

Fully responsive No app download
🔒

Privacy-First Design

Built to EU GDPR standards from the ground up. No personal data is collected for students under 18 — students use anonymous nicknames and access codes. Guardian consent is built into the onboarding flow.

GDPR compliant Guardian consent No PII stored
📊

Impact Measurement

Pre and post assessments (IA1 & IA2) track student growth across five themes: self-perception, teamwork, skills, financial awareness, and climate action. Anonymised CSV export for funder reporting.

Pre & post data 5 outcome themes Funder-ready export
👑

Admin & Oversight

The Blastbeat team has full visibility across all schools via a central admin portal. Each school operates in its own isolated space, with multi-year data structured for long-term tracking.
