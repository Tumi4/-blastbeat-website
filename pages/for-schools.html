How It Works for Your School

Three simple steps to bring real-world entrepreneurship into your classrooms.

1

Initial Consultation

2–3 weeks before term

Schedule a no-obligation call. We'll assess your school's needs and plan the programme rollout together.

2

Teacher Training

1–2 weeks before launch

Online and in-person training sessions for your staff. Full certification and ongoing support provided.

3

Programme Launch

Beginning of term

Students form ESEs and begin their journey. Our platform handles team management, progress tracking, and competition entry.

The Full-Year Programme Model

Two phases that give students the complete entrepreneurship experience.

Terms 1 & 2

Events Business Phase

  • ▶ MACC & FootBeat ESEs operate
  • ▶ Students run real events with real audiences
  • ▶ Generate actual revenue from ticket sales
  • ▶ 75% profit distributed to student teams
  • ▶ 25% pooled into climate action fund
Terms 3 & 4

Climate Action Phase

  • ▶ CAN (Climate Actions Now) ESE activates
  • ▶ Uses pooled 25% from events phase
  • ▶ Students design & execute climate projects
  • ▶ Measurable environmental impact
  • ▶ Impact reporting & community showcase

Technology Built for Schools

Every Blastbeat school gets access to a purpose-built digital platform that runs the whole programme — from student onboarding to impact reporting.

👥

Teacher Dashboard

Live student registration list, role assignments, and team overview. The platform auto-calculates the current programme week and phase — no manual tracking needed.

Live progress Role assignment Phase control
🤖

Built-In AI Mentor

Every student has a personal AI mentor embedded in the platform. They can ask it anything — what their role involves, how to complete a task, ideas for their event. No external tools needed.

Per-student AI chat Role guidance Team chat
📱

Mobile-First

Designed for students who live on their phones. The full platform — student dashboard, tasks, team chat, and teacher controls — works flawlessly on mobile and tablet.

Fully responsive No app download
🔒

Privacy-First Design

Built to EU GDPR standards from the ground up. No personal data is collected for students under 18 — students use anonymous nicknames and access codes. Guardian consent is built into the onboarding flow.

GDPR compliant Guardian consent No PII stored

Impact Measurement

Pre and post assessments (IA1 & IA2) track student growth across five themes: self-perception, teamwork, skills, financial awareness, and climate action. Anonymised CSV export available for funding and impact reporting.
