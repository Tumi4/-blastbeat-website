the invitation

Why You. Why Now.

You’ve built something. You know what it takes — the resilience, the team, the moment things click. Most young people never get to feel that. Blastbeat gives 14–18 year-olds a real business, a real event, real money and a real climate project — the closest thing to your own first break, run inside a school term.

After 23 years and 360,000 young people, the model is proven. Blastbeat V2 makes it scalable. What it needs now is a circle of people with the name, the network and the generosity to put real momentum behind it. That’s you.

what you gain

This Isn’t a Donation. It’s a Legacy.

Patrons don’t disappear into an annual report. You get a named, visible, measurable relationship with the young people you back — and the platform to tell that story.

🏆

Name a cohort, an award, a regional chapter or the national finals. “The [Your Name] Cohort” — yours, on the record, year after year.

📷

A ready-made content kit — film, photos, impact data and the students’ own Blastbeat TV episodes — so your audience sees the good you’re doing.

🎫

Access & experience

Front-row at the national finals, intimate student showcases, and the wider Blastbeat network — from Web Summit founders to the CAN group ventures.

📊

Verified impact

Every term: a report tied to your named schools — students reached, profit generated, climate projects funded, UN SDGs touched. Receipts, not vibes.

the multiplier

Give a Little. Unlock a Lot.

The real power of a patron isn’t the cheque — it’s the doors you open. Here’s how one introduction becomes a school full of changemakers.

1

You twin

Fund one or more named schools through 2winAid — or simply lend your name as a founding patron.

2

You open doors

A warm intro to two or three companies in your network. That’s all we ask — the introduction.

3

We close

Our team handles the pitch and the paperwork. Those companies buy licences via 2winAid — ESG, BBBEE, Section 18A done.

4

Everyone wins

Schools run free programmes. Companies get verified impact. You get the legacy — named, multiplied, and on the record.

Prefer a commercial arrangement? Patrons who actively unlock licence revenue can direct a finder’s credit either to fund more schools in their name, or as an affiliate fee — your call.
