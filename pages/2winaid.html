the idea

Why “Twin” Aid?

Most sponsorship is one-directional: money goes out, a logo goes on a banner. 2winAid is built so both sides win, visibly.

🏢

The Sponsor Wins

  • ✓ A named twin school — not an anonymous donation
  • ✓ Verified ESG & UN SDG reporting (4, 8, 9, 13, 17)
  • ✓ BBBEE Socio-Economic Development spend (SA)
  • ✓ Section 18A tax-deductible certificate (SA)
  • ✓ Brand on materials, event credits & Blastbeat TV
  • ✓ End-of-term impact report from their school
+
🏫

The School Wins

  • ✓ A fully-funded Blastbeat licence — free to students
  • ✓ A real Event Social Enterprise, run by their learners
  • ✓ 75% of event profit kept by the team
  • ✓ 25% funding a student-designed climate project
  • ✓ Teacher training, platform access & the AI mentor
  • ✓ A named corporate partner who actually shows up
how the twinning works

Four Steps to a Twin

1

Choose a tier

Twin a single school for a pilot half-year (€1,250) or commit to a full year or a cluster of schools. The bigger the commitment, the deeper the reporting.

2

Get matched

We pair you with a named school that fits your region, sector or B-BBEE goals. You meet your twin before term one.

3

They run the programme

Your twin school forms its ESE, runs a real event, keeps 75% of profit and funds a climate project with the other 25%. You get progress updates on the admin dashboard.

4

You get the receipts

End of term: a verifiable impact report, the Section 18A / BBBEE paperwork, photos, the Blastbeat TV episode, and the financial summary — all tied to your named school.

The 20-School Pilot

Twenty founding schools. Free €1,250 six-month licences. Each one twinned with a 2winAid sponsor. This is how we prove the model at scale.

Pilot Places Twinned 6 / 20

14 schools still need a twin sponsor for the 2026 cohort. Each placement is €1,250 for the full six-month pilot — the school pays nothing.

🎓

Who qualifies

Secondary schools, FET colleges and community youth groups working with 14–18s (and 14–34 for sports clubs). Priority to under-resourced communities.

⏳

What “6 months” means

A full half-year licence — one complete ESE cycle from team formation to live event and impact report. Roughly half the €2,500 annual rate.

💰

Who pays

A 2winAid sponsor funds each licence. Schools and students pay nothing. Sponsors get the full ESG / BBBEE / 18A return package.
