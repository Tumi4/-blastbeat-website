What is FootBeat?

FootBeat is the sports enterprise vertical of the Blastbeat programme. Students organise and run real sports events — primarily 5-a-side football tournaments — while learning every aspect of sports business management.

Supported by Arsenal Community Foundation and West Ham United Foundation, FootBeat brings the Premier League's community sports ethos into secondary education worldwide.

What FootBeat Students Run

5-a-Side League

Multi-week league with tables, fixtures, and weekly matchdays. Students run the full admin, refereeing, and venue operations.

Knockout Cup

Single-day or weekend cup competition with bracket draws, match-day programmes, and prize ceremonies.

School Championships

Inter-school or inter-house championships. Multiple teams, structured fixtures, sponsorship, media coverage, trophies.

Coaching Clinic

Ticketed skills workshops and coaching days for younger students. Students sell spots, bring in coaches, manage logistics.

Sports Awards Night

End-of-season ticketed awards event. Best player, fair play, top scorer. Students produce the whole ceremony from invites to stage.

Blastbeat TV — Sports

Weekly sports content episode — match highlights, interviews, behind-the-scenes. Submitted to the national competition.
