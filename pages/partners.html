the model

Free for Schools.
Funded by You.

The Adopt-A-School model removes cost as a barrier to youth entrepreneurship. Companies cover the licence — schools and students pay nothing.

🏪

School Lists Need

A school registers interest and lists their programme needs on our marketplace — visible to all potential sponsors.

🤝

You Adopt & Fund

Your company selects a school (or bundle of schools), pays the licence, and becomes a named sponsor of that school’s ESE journey.

🌟

Impact Delivered

Students run real events. You receive a verified impact report, co-branded recognition, and measurable ESG outcomes.

“For the cost of one company team-building day, you transform an entire school for a year.”
