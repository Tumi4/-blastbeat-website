🌱 Climate Actions Now group

Climate Actions Now

Every Event Social Enterprise commits 25% of its profits to student-designed climate action projects. This is not a suggestion — it is built into the DNA of the Blastbeat model.

Climate Actions Now (CAN) is a UK registered charity (No. 1113530) that receives, manages, and deploys these funds toward real environmental projects designed and chosen by students.
