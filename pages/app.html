what it does

One App, the Whole ESE

Everything the analogue programme did with binders and facilitators — now phase-gated, AI-guided and tracked in real time.

🤖

AI mentor “Beat”

Every student gets a personal AI guide, trained on 23 years of Blastbeat, that answers questions on any task, any time.

🎯

Role × phase tasks

Each of the 14 roles sees only the tasks relevant to them and the current phase. The teacher controls the phase gate.

💰

Live profit tracking

Real income and costs logged as they happen, with the 75/25 split calculated automatically and shown to the whole team.

🌱

Climate-action log

The Climate Lead designs, runs and measures the project funded by the 25% — photos, metrics and outcomes in one place.

📱

Blastbeat TV

Upload weekly clips and the final 10-minute episode straight from the app. Build a real portfolio while you go.

🎓

Verifiable credentials

Students graduate with a W3C-aligned digital licence proving the role they held and the skills they demonstrated.

🏫

Students

Join with a QR code — no download, no email. Pick your role, work your tasks, ask Beat anything, and watch the profit grow.

🦞

Teachers

A live class dashboard. Move the cohort through phases, see who’s on track, and let the AI mentor handle the “how do I…?” questions. Facilitate, don’t lecture.

🏢

Sponsors

Watch your twinned school’s progress in real time, then export a verifiable impact report for ESG, BBBEE and Section 18A.

availability

Live for the 2026 Cohort

Blastbeat V2 goes live 18 May 2026 with founding schools in Cape Town and Kigali. Web app first — iOS and Android to follow. Get on the early-access list through your school application.
